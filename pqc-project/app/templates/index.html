Post-Quantum Cryptography

A Demonstration of Next-Generation Encryption

Welcome

Welcome to our demonstration of Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC). In a world where quantum computers threaten to break our current encryption standards, PQC offers a new generation of cryptographic algorithms that are resistant to quantum attacks.

This project showcases PQC in action by running two parallel servers:

  • Server A (Classic): Uses traditional cryptography, which is vulnerable to quantum attacks.
  • Server B (PQC-Enabled): Uses PQC to protect data, both in transit and at rest.
